Restaurants

My favorite restaurants in Richmond

Edo's Squid

Casual brick-lined Italian restaurant with a full bar & plenty of calamari dishes on the menu.

Address:

411 N Harrison St

What I like about it

Edo's Squid serves amazing Italian food.

Learn more

Patio Thai

Snug venue with a courtyard garden offering traditional fried rice, curry & noodle dishes.

Address:

103 E Cary St

What I like about it

There's a nice patio for outdoor seating. They have the best Thai food in Richmond.

Learn more

Ipanema Cafe

Vegetarian & vegan eats come in quaint digs with white brick walls, live music & a patio.

Address:

917 W Grace St

What I like about it

Ipanema Cafe has cozy seating and great vegetarian food.
